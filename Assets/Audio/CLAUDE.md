# Audio

## Purpose
Sound effects and music files.

## Conventions
- PascalCase names (e.g., `Explosion_Large.wav`, `MainTheme.ogg`)
- Organize in subfolders: `SFX/`, `Music/`
- WAV for short sound effects, OGG for music/longer clips
- Configure compression settings per platform in import settings
